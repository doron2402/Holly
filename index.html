OUR APPROACH

We that believe children learn by doing.

INVESTIGATE

We inspire children to learn by creating an engaging environment full of learning opportunities. We encourage children to explore, investigate, create and discover their passions. We believe that if they are treasured by the people they spend their time with and they are excited by the activities available to them, they will reach for the stars.

CREATE

Our goal is to bring forth the passions and curiosity already within each child by introducing ideas and concepts that inspire. Our bright, airy classrooms are packed with materials to engage and support creativity and curiosity. We draw from the experts in the field including Maria Montessori, The Reggio-Emilia Approach, Waldorf and more. We don’t adhere rigidly to any single system.

DISCOVER

At Rockridge Little School each child’s initiative and independence is recognized, celebrated, and supported. Children will have the opportunity to try out a number of structured learning activities, and have access to special materials that emphasize the use of all senses as they learn about the world around them.

OUR STORY

Holly & Rockridge Little School

  • 2004

    The Dream

    In 2005, after a 20-year career in the non-profit field, Holly pursued her dream of opening a preschool.

  • 2005

    A School is Born

    The College Avenue School serves forty children and is located in the heart of Rockridge in the historic College Avenue Presbyterian Church, designed by Julia Morgan.

  • 2012

    A New School is Added

    In 2012, after 37 years, The Rainbow School closed its doors giving RLS the opportunity to add a second school. The Taft Avenue School serves thirty children and is located right off Broadway Avenue in an Arts & Crafts style bungalow.

  • 2014

    The Community Approves

    In 2014 our community voted Rockridge Little School The Best Independent Preschool.

Our Teacher

Little School teachers are dedicated to introducing children to the marvelous possibility the world and its inhabitants have to offer.

We hire professionals who love children and have committed themselves to making the world a better place for them. They are compelling people who are passionate about helping children discover the wonders of the world and of their own growing capabilities. Our small teacher:student ratio allows teachers to give each child the individual attention he or she deserves. Teachers exceed mandated standards for early childhood educators, and they take advantage of learning opportunities for professional development. They will ensure that every child feels gifted, respected, responsible, and profoundly cherished regardless of any aspect of their life that makes them feel “different.”
